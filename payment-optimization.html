Payment process optimization

Role Product designer
Timeline 2 Months

The project

Delivered a streamlined, consistent payment experience that improved usability and aligned the journey with modern product standards.

The objective of this project was to redesign the payment journey for the web platform of Banco do Brasil. Although the flow already existed in a legacy environment, the platform’s evolution required a deeper review of interactions, usability gaps, and user expectations to ensure a more seamless and modern experience. Building on a previously defined strategic solution from the former design team, my role was to translate that vision into a fully realized interface—structuring information, refining interaction patterns, and applying a consistent design language aligned with the platform’s standards. This included improving clarity across steps, reducing cognitive load, and ensuring the journey was intuitive, accessible, and scalable for future enhancements.
