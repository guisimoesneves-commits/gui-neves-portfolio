FADa - Data Analysis Tool

Role Product designer
Timeline 6 Months

The project

FADa is a dashboard that helps chatbot curators easily explore and optimize conversation data.

FADa, or, data analysis tool, is a dashboard created for the chatbot curatorship department of Banco do Brasil. The intention was to allow that the users to see all the chatbot interactions with the final clients and work on top of it. The objective was to provide all the necessary information to the user in a clear way, and allow them to have full control over the data being displayed, performing filtering actions, manipulating charts, and much more. I was the main designer responsible for this project since its conception, the work was conducted alongside with a developer and a couple of people from the curatorship team, that way we could consult them whenever we needed and perform some quick test to validate hypothesis.

The project began with user interviews to deeply understand the needs and pain points of chatbot curators, followed by the creation of wireframes and user flows to map out key interactions. Early concepts were tested with users to validate assumptions and refine the direction before moving into visual design. I then designed the initial screens, gathered feedback through iterative validation cycles, and expanded the solution to cover the full experience. To ensure consistency and scalability, I concluded the process by documenting components, color styles, and design patterns into a reusable library, enabling more efficient future iterations and alignment across the team.

The result

The final result was a robust and flexible data analysis platform that gave chatbot curators full visibility and control over user interactions. The tool enabled users to easily read, review, and classify conversations using customizable tags, making it simpler to organize large volumes of data and identify patterns. Powerful filtering capabilities allowed users to segment interactions by date, source, intention, and subject, helping them quickly narrow down specific scenarios and uncover actionable insights with precision.

In addition, the platform introduced simulation features that allowed curators to test the chatbot’s accuracy in controlled scenarios, validating improvements before deploying them live. Performance metrics were presented through clear, customizable dashboards, enabling users to track success rates, identify bottlenecks, and continuously optimize the chatbot experience. This combination of deep analysis, flexibility, and usability resulted in a tool that significantly improved decision-making and operational efficiency.
